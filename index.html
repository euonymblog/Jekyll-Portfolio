About me

SEO, website, social media, newsletters... nowadays there are so many ways to communicate. Finding the time as a small business owner or NGO can be frustrating - you know you need to do this stuff, but if you take the time to do it, you're not getting your core work done. That's where the One Woman Comms Department comes in. I can work with you to identify, design and provide the services your organisation needs. I've worked as a media spokesperson for an international organisation, in public communication roles and consulting into small organisations like yours. I am familiar with website design and management, writing, editing, talking to journalists and making the most of social media - all the things you need to have someone take care of. So please browse my site, take a look at my skills and experience and if you like what you see, get in touch!
